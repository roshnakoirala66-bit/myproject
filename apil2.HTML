Dashain

Dashain is the biggest and most important hindu festival celebrate in Nepal. It usally lasts for fifteen days and synbolize the victory of good over evil. The festival horons goddes durga's triumph over the demon mahishasura. During Dashain, families come together to celebrate , and many people return to their hometowns to spend time with their loved ones. Elder put tika and jamara on the forehead of younger family member as blessings for good heaith, prosperity and happiness.
