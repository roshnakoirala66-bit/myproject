Dashain,Tihar and Chaat-Festival of Nepal

Nepal is a country rich in culture and traditions. Among many celebrations, Dashain ,Tihar, and chhat are three majour festivals.These festivals bring happiness, togetherness, and devotion among people. Dashain, tihar and chaath are important festival in Nepal. Dashain celebrate the victory of good over evil with family gathering and blessings Tihar, the festival of lights, honors animals , gods, and family with lamps and decorations. Chhath is dedicated to the sun god ,with prayers and fasting for heaith and prosperity . These festival show the rich culture and traditions of Nepal.
